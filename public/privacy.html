Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Auto Email Sender ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our email sending service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture

2.2 Email Data

When you send emails through our service, we temporarily process:

  • Recipient email addresses
  • Email subject lines
  • Email message content
  • File attachments

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity through Google OAuth
  • Send emails on your behalf using the Gmail API
  • Store your templates and files for convenience
  • Improve our service

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • We do not store your Google password
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely in encrypted sessions
  • Email content is not permanently stored on our servers
  • File attachments are stored locally and can be deleted by you

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication
  • Gmail API: For sending emails on your behalf

Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your stored files and templates
  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time
  • Request deletion of your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Session data: Deleted after logout or 24 hours of inactivity
  • Templates: Stored until you delete them
  • Uploaded files: Stored until you delete them

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@autoemail.com
